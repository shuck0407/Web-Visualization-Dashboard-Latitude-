Summary: Weather Behavior vs Latitude

The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you approach the Earth's equator. To accomplish this analysis, we downloaded a full list of cities from the OpenWeatherMap website. Then, we generated a random list of 300 cities from the Northern Hemisphere and 300 cities from the Southern Hemisphere.

After assembling the city dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot various aspects of the weather vs. latitude. Factors we looked at included: Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness and Wind Speed. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, as well as explanations and descriptions of any trends and correlations witnessed.
